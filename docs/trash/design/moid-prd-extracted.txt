1. Executive Summary
MO!D is an AI-powered manufacturing intelligence platform designed to transform fragmented,
inconsistent, and non-standard manufacturing quality data into actionable operational intelligence.
The platform ingests unstructured spreadsheets, reconstructs a mathematically verifiable operational
model of the factory, identifies quality losses, determines probable root causes, generates Lean Six
Sigma diagnostics, estimates financial impact, and recommends improvement actions.
Unlike traditional dashboards, MO!D is not a reporting tool.
It is a manufacturing intelligence and diagnostic operating system.

3. Product Philosophy
Principle 1
Never modify customer data.
Original uploaded files remain immutable.
MO!D creates derived interpretations.
Principle 2
Never trust spreadsheet formulas.
All calculations must be reconstructed independently.
Totals, percentages and formulas are treated as references, not truth.
Principle 3
Factories are different.
The system must never assume:
fixed process order
fixed defect names
fixed column names
fixed reporting formats
The platform must learn the structure.
Principle 4
Every insight must be explainable.
Every dashboard number must be traceable back to:
